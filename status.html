There are three areas that provide information about the status of KISSmetrics and the status of your account:

Site Description
https://www.kissmetrics.com/settings Check how much of your data we've processed, for your reports.
http://status.kissmetrics.com Check for any widespread issues with KISSmetrics.
http://uptime.kissmetrics.com/ See the history of our services' uptime.
